As an easy-to-access facility and an open cultural space, POSCO Art Museum aims to enrich lives with Art. The museum which “moves the world in silence,” we make efforts to discover, support, and develop precious and meaningful cultural art as a POSCO’s spreader of cultural value.

POSCO Art Museum has three visions, ‘Creative’, ‘Share’, and ‘Dignity’. The museum is as ‘Creative museum’, spreading POSCO’s company culture through art and we should be settled the ‘Sharing museum’, sharing and communicating art with everyone. Additionally, we are also making an effort to enjoy cultural art with everybody beyond age or gender as ‘Dignity museum’ harmonizing with global company POSCO.
